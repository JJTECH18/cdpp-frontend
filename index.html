Who We Are

Camp David Prayer Power (CDPP) is more than a church — it is a movement. Born from a burning desire to see God's Kingdom made manifest on earth, we have grown from twelve into hundreds, united in faith, prayer, and love.

For over a decade, we have carried one conviction: that prayer is not merely a religious duty, but the most powerful force available to humanity.

We believe in signs, wonders, and miracles — and we have witnessed them. The story of CDPP is written in transformed lives.

Our Mission

To raise a generation of prayer warriors who transform their world through the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

A world where every person encounters the living God and walks in their divine purpose.
